## We find where your phone leads die. Then we fix it.

Most trade websites lose customers before they ever call — slow load, no phone button, a form
that goes nowhere. We show you exactly where that's happening, for free, in a video. Then, if you
want it fixed, we build it.

### Step 1 — Free Teardown (no cost, no obligation)

Send us your current website. Within **24 hours** you get:

- A short, plain-English breakdown of the 3 biggest things costing you calls
- A quick video walking through it

No pitch required to get this. If your site's fine, we'll tell you that too.

### Step 2 — Pick a Build Tier

| Tier | Price | What it's for |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic Starter / Refresh** | from **$500** | A simple, credible one-page site with fast lead capture — good for a quick fix or a brand-new small operation. |
| **Business Site Package** | **$1,000–$2,000** | A full local/regional company site — proof of work, services, intake form, deployed and live. |
| **Premium Custom** | **Quote only, $2,000+** | Larger builds: multiple service lines, portals, online payments, integrations with your other tools. Scoped and quoted individually. |

A **20% deposit (nonrefundable)** starts the build. We'll tell you the timeline for your specific
project before you pay it — it depends on scope, not a fixed calendar promise.

### Step 3 — What Happens After You Say Yes

1. We scope the build against your tier and confirm timeline.
2. We build it — mobile-first, because that's where most of your customers are.
3. Before handoff, we run the same audit tool that found your original issues against the new
   site, to confirm the fixes actually landed.
4. We wire up lead alerting so a new customer inquiry doesn't sit unread in an inbox.
5. You're live.

### Optional — Ongoing Monitoring *(new offer, ask about availability)*

For businesses that want their site checked on a recurring basis rather than a one-time build, we
offer a monitoring add-on. This is a newer part of the service — ask directly if you're interested
and we'll confirm current pricing and availability rather than assuming a fixed monthly rate here.
